About Me
=====
I'm Max Hartman, an incoming Master of Science in Electrical & Computer Engineering student at UIUC. I completed my BS at UIUC in Computer Engineering with a minor in Mathematics in May 2025. 

I'm a Software Development Engineer Intern at Amazon, working on AWS LoggingCore Observability. I previous was a founding engineer at [Electrokare](https://www.electrokare.com/).

Research Interests
=====
As AI models grow in scale and capability, understanding how they reason becomes as critical as what they can do. My research focuses on developing interpretability methods that explain the internal mechanisms of mutlimodal language models, so we can understand why specific predictions are made and leverage those results to improve the efficiency of the underlying models. To this end, my current interests mainly lie in Generative Models, Interpretability, AI Safety, Representation Learning, and Federated Learning.
