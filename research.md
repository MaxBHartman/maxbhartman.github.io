# Research

## Interests
My research interests lie in Representation Learning, Language Models, and Machine Learning more broadly. I am particularly focused on how machine learning models can represent and integrate vast, complex knowledge about the world to enhance their decision-making and reasoning capabilities. By leveraging meta-learning techniques, I aim to explore how large language models (LLMs) can learn more efficiently across different tasks and domains, adapting quickly with minimal supervision. My goal is to create AI systems that are not only capable of learning continually but also able to generalize world knowledge to novel situations, enabling autonomous decision-making in new environments.
